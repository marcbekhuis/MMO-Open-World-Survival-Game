Mountain Range

The Mountain Range is a high-altitude biome of snow-capped peaks, steep passes, and deep ravines. High winds, sudden storms, and thin air make travel hazardous without proper equipment.

Features

- Mineral-rich veins and caves containing rare ores and crystals.
- Harsh weather that affects visibility and movement.
- Isolated highland settlements and ancient shrines.

Hazards

- Avalanches, chasms, and falling rocks.
- Cold exposure mechanics and thin-air penalties for low-endurance players.
