# Mountain Range

The Mountain Range is a high-altitude biome of snow-capped peaks, steep passes, and deep ravines. High winds, sudden storms, and thin air make travel hazardous without proper equipment.

Home to many dwarven settlements

## Features

- Mineral-rich veins and caves containing rare ores and crystals.
- Harsh weather that affects visibility, stamina, and movement.
- Isolated highland settlements, ancient shrines, and watch-fortresses.
- Natural chokepoints that make roads, bridges, and passes strategically important.

## Hazards

- Avalanches, chasms, and falling rocks.
- Cold exposure mechanics and thin-air penalties for low-endurance players.
- Long sightlines that expose travellers to flying threats, ranged attackers, and weather changes.

## Fauna

- stone giant
- mountain goats
