Bamboo Jungle

The Bamboo Jungle is a dense, vertical biome dominated by towering bamboo groves. Its tall, cylindrical trunks create narrow sightlines and natural vertical pathways that favor stealth and ambush tactics.

Features

- Narrow corridors formed by bamboo stalks, with canopy walkways and root bridges
- Specialized resources: bamboo fiber, reed sap, and lightweight timber used in crafting
- Predators that blend with the vertical environment (e.g., Bamboo Spiders)

Fauna

- Bamboo Spider — a camouflaged ambush predator (see [Bamboo spider](../Creatures/Bamboo-spider.md))

See also: [Creatures index](../Creatures.md)

Hazards

- Ambush zones and concealed traps
- Slippery terrain near river-fed clearings
