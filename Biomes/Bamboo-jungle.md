# Bamboo Jungle

The Bamboo Jungle is a dense, vertical biome dominated by towering bamboo groves. Its tall, cylindrical trunks create narrow sightlines and natural vertical pathways that favor stealth, ambush tactics, and fast repositioning.

## Features

- Narrow corridors formed by bamboo stalks, with canopy walkways and root bridges.
- Specialized resources such as bamboo fibre, reed sap, and lightweight timber used in crafting.
- Predators that blend with the vertical environment, especially the [Bamboo spider](../Creatures/Bamboo-spider.md).
- Small hidden villages built on stilts, lashed platforms, or suspended bridges above flood-prone ground.

## Hazards

- Ambush zones and concealed traps.W
- Sudden visibility shifts when wind moves dense bamboo screens.

## Fauna

- [Bamboo spider](../Creatures/Bamboo-spider.md) - A camouflaged ambush predator that uses vertical surfaces and dense stalks to close distance.
