# Swamp Biome

The Swamp is a dense, humid biome of blackwater channels, reedy marshes, and half-sunken groves. Visibility is often poor beneath the heavy canopy, and the air carries the scent of peat and rot. It should feel slow, watchful, and dangerous: a place where the ground itself cannot always be trusted.

## Features

- Waterlogged terrain that slows travel and favors amphibious movement.
- Abundant resources for alchemical crafting: medicinal herbs, fungal nodes, rare resins, and water-filtering reeds.
- Half-submerged ruins that can hide puzzle chambers, flooded cellars, or forgotten shrines.
- Narrow boat routes that reward players who invest in local knowledge or [Cartography](../Skills/Artisan/Cartography.md).

## Hazards

- Toxic plants and fog that can apply temporary debuffs.
- Quicksand, sinkholes, and concealed drop-offs in poorly charted areas.
- Predators that use water, reeds, and mud banks to break line of sight before striking.

## Fauna

- Alligator
- Swamp spider
- Large water snakes
- Titan turtle
