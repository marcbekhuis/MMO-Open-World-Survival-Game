Swamp biome

The Swamp is a dense, humid biome of blackwater channels, reedy marshes, and half-sunken groves. Visibility is often poor beneath the heavy canopy, and the air carries the scent of peat and rot.

Features

- Waterlogged terrain that slows travel and favors amphibious movement.
- Abundant resources for alchemical crafting: medicinal herbs, fungal nodes, and rare resins.
- Home to stealthy predators and poisonous flora.

Hazards

- Toxic plants and fog that can apply temporary debuffs.
- Quicksand and sinkholes in poorly charted areas.
