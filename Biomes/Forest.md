Forest biome

The Forest biome is a temperate mixed-woodland region that sits between plains and deeper wildlands. It provides abundant resources, shelter, and lore-rich ruins for early to mid-game players.

Features

- Harvestable timber, wild game, and foraging nodes
- Hidden groves, old cairns, and minor puzzle ruins
- Travelling merchant routes and small cabin settlements

Hazards

- Territorial predators and occasional bandit activity
- Dense undergrowth that hides traps and ambush points
