# Floating Islands

The Floating Islands are a stunning, mystical biome of landmasses suspended high above the ground, seemingly in defiance of gravity. They are held aloft by massive mana crystal ores embedded deep within them, attuned to a specific magical frequency that keeps the islands and everything fixed to them airborne. These foundational crystals are indestructible and cannot be mined, which is what guarantees the islands their perpetual stability, and reaching this biome at all is one of the late-game achievements the world holds in reserve.

## Terrain and Mana Crystals

Each island is unique, ranging from small drifting fragments to expansive landmasses carrying lush vegetation and cascading waterfalls that evaporate into mist long before they could reach the ground far below. The same mana that lifts the islands saturates everything on them, giving the biome its luminous, otherworldly character. Beyond the indestructible foundation crystals, smaller and less stable mana crystals can be found scattered across the islands, holding significant magical energy without threatening the structures that depend on the larger ores.

## Resources

The harvestable crystals are the islands' great prize. They feed the enchanting and spell traditions of [Magic](../Magic.md) and the high tiers of [Crafting](../Crafting.md), and because they can be gathered without destabilising the islands, they offer a rare, renewable source of concentrated magical power to any player able to reach them. That accessibility is the whole problem, since the resource is rich but the road to it is the hardest in the world.

## Creatures

The signature inhabitants of the biome are the [Flying Leviathans](../Creatures/Flying-leviathan.md), slow and majestic sky-whales bound to the islands by the same mana that lifts the land. Their young depend on the islands' concentrated energy to survive, which keeps the species tied to the biome and makes an encounter with one a defining moment of any expedition into the high country.

## Access and Travel

Reaching the floating islands is a challenge in its own right and a test of everything the travel systems offer. Players ascend by climbing and grappling, or by taming the flying creatures described in [Travel and Mounts](../Travel-and-mounts.md), with a tamed Flying Leviathan the grandest route of all. Once aloft, the islands reward the effort: their stable ground and unmatched isolation make them prime sites for the kind of aerial foothold the [Building System](../Building-system.md) supports, a base no ground-bound raider can easily reach.
