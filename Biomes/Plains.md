The Plains serve as one of the beginner-friendly biomes, characterized by vast open grasslands that stretch as far as the eye can see. These rolling fields are dotted with occasional flowerbeds, adding bursts of color to the serene landscape. Rivers flowing down from distant mountains weave through the plains, their banks lined with slightly denser foliage, creating lush oases amidst the expansive grasslands.
These pockets of greenery are teeming with life, serving as habitats for herds of grazing animals and other wildlife that thrive in the gentle environment. The tranquil beauty of the plains, combined with its abundant resources and relative safety, makes it an ideal starting point for adventurers beginning their journey.
