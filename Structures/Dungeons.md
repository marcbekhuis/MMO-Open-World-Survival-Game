Dungeons are ancient, indestructible superstructures that defy the laws of physics, appearing far larger on the inside than their exterior suggests. These enigmatic structures are home to powerful monsters, which, while formidable, pale in comparison to the creatures that roam the world outside.
The deeper players venture into these massive dungeons, the greater the rewards they can uncover. Treasures of immense value await those who dare to explore, alongside significant amounts of experience points to aid in progression. However, the challenges within grow increasingly perilous, testing the skill and resolve of any who enter.
