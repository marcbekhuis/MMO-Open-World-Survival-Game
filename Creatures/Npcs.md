Npcs are humanoid that use the same 3d models as players do they are dwarves, elves and humans.

Npcs wander the world just like players to make the world feel like its inhabited by several civilizations. Merchant npcs travel araound from city to city requesting escort missions to make sure their goods arive safely at there destinations these goods could be items, live stock or other npcs and players.

Larger cities have mage npcs that protect the cities and use the magic spell [Nature growth](../\Magic\Spells\Earth\Nature-growth.md) to restore resources around the city when they are over populated with players that are gathering resources, to make sure all players get a change to collect the resources they need.

Cities also have knights and archers that protect them and keep the players in order, their equipment and weapons strenght is based on the cities size and strenght.

There are also many other jobs/roles in the cities like farmers, bartenders, musicians/bards, blacksmithes, traders, weavers, butchers, builders. all these jobs/roles help make the cities feel alive as all Npcs perform there tasks actively and visibly.

Child npcs can also be found around cities running and playing around the city interacting with other npcs and players.
