Kraken

The Kraken is an apex sea creature that dwells in the deepest ocean trenches and occasionally surfaces to challenge ships and coastal settlements. It is a rare, world-event tier encounter designed for coordinated groups.

Behavior & mechanics

- Deep-water ambush: prefers darkness and pressure-filled trenches; surfaces to attack ships.
- Tentacle mechanics: area grabs, ship-stunning impulses, and localized downward pressure that affects sailing.
- Weak points: the crusted carapace reveals soft tissue patches after sustained damage, allowing focused strike windows.

Rewards & role

- High-value drops include rare crafting materials, mana-rich organs, and salvageable hull plating from ships.
- Kraken encounters are intended as emergent world events that require cooperation across multiple parties.
