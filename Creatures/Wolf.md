Wolves serve as one of the game’s grounded and familiar early-game enemies, providing players with a natural and recognizable challenge. Unlike many creatures in other games, wolves in this world behave as true pack hunters, leveraging their numbers and coordination to outsmart and overwhelm their prey.
Wolves are not solitary creatures; they hunt and fight together as a unit, making encounters with them unique and engaging. Players will need to be cautious when venturing into wolf territory, as their pack mentality means they will work together to encircle, flank, and wear down their targets. This dynamic behavior adds depth to combat, requiring players to strategize and prioritize threats within the group.

Story Hook

On the edge of a northern encampment, the old hunter Edrin kept a carved wolf bone talisman. Villagers believed it kept the pack at bay. When Edrin vanished, the talisman was found cracked beside a series of pawprints leading into the pines. Some say the pack took him; others say he ran with them and learned their ways. Either tale is told to children as caution and legend.
