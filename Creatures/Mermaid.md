The mermaid is a sea creature that lures sailors into the depth of the sea, they sing a song that charms anyone that hears it attracting there victim into the sea once close enough the mermaid will grap the victim and pull them to the bottom of the sea to let them drown. To anyone under the influence of the charm the mermaid looks like the perfect beautiful woman with a fish tail scales reflecting in rainbow colors, but when the charm is broken this fake front disappears and their true form is visible the the beautiful human upper half turn into a terrifying fish humanoid with gills on the side of there chest. The tail that used to look like a rainbow of color also turns into just a grey skin of colors all the beauty of this creature was caused by its charm. Any sailor with a high enough intelligence stat or with the right equipment can resist the charm. When a mermaid notices that the charm is not working or it was broken they will either lash out or flee into the depths of the water. While they do not often use it mermaids can speak the global language and are just as intelligent as a human. All mermaids are female, they reproduce by mating with their male victims
