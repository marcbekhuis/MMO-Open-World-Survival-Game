# Design Backlog

This is a working file, not part of the design proper. It carries context between sessions so that ideas, questions, and deferred decisions survive even though AI assistants do not share memory. Everything recorded here is provisional: items move out of the backlog and into the design documents as they are written, answered, or decided. See [SHARED-INSTRUCTIONS.md](SHARED-INSTRUCTIONS.md) for how this file fits the wider workflow.
