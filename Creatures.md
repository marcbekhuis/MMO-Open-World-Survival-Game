# Creatures

Creatures define the living pressure of the world. They are enemies, resources, ambience, quest anchors, and ecological signals that help each biome feel distinct. Small creatures make travel readable, while apex creatures create stories that groups remember.

## Design Role

Creature design should connect behaviour, biome, and reward. A forest predator should teach different habits from a mountain giant or ocean apex creature. Whenever possible, creature mechanics should create decisions around preparation, terrain, group roles, and observation instead of relying only on larger health pools.
