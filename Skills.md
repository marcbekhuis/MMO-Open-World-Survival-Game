Skills overview

Skills define active and passive abilities learned and improved through use and investment. Skills are grouped into trees or families such as Combat, Artisan, Gathering, and Magic. Each skill unlocks new mechanics, upgrades, or specializations.

Primary skill families

- [Combat](Skills/Combat.md): Weapon-based active abilities and defensive maneuvers.
- Artisan: Crafting, refinement, and production skills (see `Skills/Artisan/` for specific artisan skills such as Cartography).
- Gathering: Resource-focused skills that improve yield and efficiency.
- Magic: Spellcasting and arcane disciplines (see [Magic](Magic.md) for spells and rules).

Progression model

- Skills improve through use and dedicated training resources.
- Skill points and unlock tiers enable branching specializations.
